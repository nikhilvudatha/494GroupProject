NBA Visualization

1.) In order to utilize all the visualizations properly, select two teams you would like to compare stats of.

2.) Once Choose, click on a bar within the bar chart and this will bring up a corresponding swarm chart which will allow for more in depth analyzation of the teams stats.

3.) Additionally, all the rings around the teams logos are each of the free throw, mid range as well as the three point percentage of that team.

4.) Finally, the line chart shows more comparative data and will indicate where certain stats overlap in order to show similarities or strong suits of another team
